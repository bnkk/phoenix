Phoenix Project

The Phoenix Project was written in an attempt to try and create a very simple and easy to understand programming language that novices and experts can pick up with ease. Phoenix is built to be 100% free and open source with no strings attached. We do not collect any of your data and we welcome suggestions and contributions to the project.

For questions related to the use of Phoenix, please consult these web pages and the Phoenix docs. If that fails, the help@phoenix.ga email address might be useful. Comments on these web pages and the development of Phoenix are welcome on our developer list at devs@phoenix.ga All of our lists have public archives.
